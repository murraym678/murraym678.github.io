GENE30004 Drosophila Development Practical v100

On this website you will encounter virtual slides of embryos which will be used to answer questions in your Prac Worksheet. The embryos are of two genotypes.

Genotype-A = gene1- / TM3 gene2-regulatory-element::lacZ

Genotype-B = gene3- / TM3 gene4-regulatory-element::lacZ

The primary goal is to identify genes 1,2,3 and 4 from this list:

  • prospero (pros)
  • fushi-tarazu (ftz)
  • knirps (kni)
  • hunchback (hb)
