GENE30004 Drosophila Development Practical - 2020

On this website you will encounter virtual slides of embryos which will be used to answer questions in your Prac Worksheet. The embryos are of two genotypes.

   Genotype-A = gene1- / TM3 gene2-regulatory-element::lacZ

   Genotype-B = gene3- / TM3 gene4-regulatory-element::lacZ

The mystery you are trying to solve is: what is the identity of genes 1,2,3 and 4?

Each will be one of the genes in this list:

  • prospero (pros)
  • fushi-tarazu (ftz)
  • knirps (kni)
  • hunchback (hb)

    • To begin, please enter your student ID and hit the Submit button.

    This will create randomly generated slides that are specific to you. i.e. you must use your own slide for this - don't copy off a friend!

    WEEK 1 - WILDTYPE & GENOTYPE A

    During week 1 you will look at wild type embryos stained for EVE to get to know the normal pattern of expression.

    Go to the wild type page.

    You will then look at embryos that are derived from genotypeA parents. There are two slides to look at. The first is one in which only beta-Galactosidase antibody has been used. The second uses two primary antibodies - anti-EVE and anti-betaGAL. With these clues you should be able to determine genotype A.

    Go to the slide: Genotype-A     beta-GAL/AP
